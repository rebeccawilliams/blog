    About

    Rebecca Williams was born and raised in Springfield, Massachusetts and has spent time living throughout Western Massachusetts, Boston, New York City, and now, Washington D.C. But in ways she also lives on the Internet.

    Rebecca is an information policy analyst with a background in law and city planning. By day she works as a Digital Services Expert at the White House Office of Management and Budget. By night she serves on the board of Legal Hackers and co-organizes the DC Legal Hackers chapter.

    She spends a lot of time thinking about systems, language, and the future. She enjoys biopics, art museums, and noodles.
